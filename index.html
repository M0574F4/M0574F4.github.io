Research

I am interested in computer vision, image and signal processing, multimodal models, and communication systems. My research bridges these fields, focusing on leveraging AI to enhance imaging, communication in noisy environment, and signal processing techniques.

Learning-Based Image Compression for Wireless Communications
M. Naseri, P. Ashtari, M. Seif, E. De Poorter, H. V. Poor, A. Shahid
IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communications, Under Review, 2024
Project Page / arXiv

This project explores a learning-based approach to image compression tailored for wireless communication environments. It evaluates the effects on reliability, throughput, and latency, aiming to enhance the efficiency of image transmission over noisy channels.

High-Throughput Blind Co-Channel Interference Cancellation for Edge Devices
M. Naseri, E. De Poorter, I. Moerman, H. V. Poor, A. Shahid
IEEE Open Journal of the Communications Society, Under Revision, 2024
Project Page / arXiv

This project presents a high-throughput method for blind co-channel interference cancellation tailored for edge devices. Utilizing depthwise separable convolutions, quantization, and pruning techniques, it significantly enhances interference management in wireless communications.

Machine Learning-Based Angle of Arrival Estimation for Ultra-Wideband Radios
M. Naseri, A. Shahid, G. Gordebeke, S. Lemey, M. Boes, S. Van de Velde, E. De Poorter
IEEE Communications Letters, Vol. 26, No. 6, pp. 1273-1277, 2022
Project Page / arXiv

This project develops a machine learning-based approach for Angle of Arrival (AoA) estimation in Ultra-Wideband (UWB) radios. By leveraging advanced neural network architectures, it enhances the accuracy and reliability of AoA measurements in complex environments.
